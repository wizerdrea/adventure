Your Goal: survive!

In adventure you will be presented with a series of choices, such as "up or down", "right or left" or "in or out". However, as you can see these choices don't provide much context as to what you are actually choosing, so your chalenge is to make the choice to survive. If you're lucky you might just make it!!
